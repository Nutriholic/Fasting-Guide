Fasting Diet Guide

1. Replacement of Grains

  • Millets: Foxtail, Kodo, Proso, Barnyard, Little Millet, Pearl Millet, Browntop Millet.
  • Seeds: Amaranth, Quinoa, Pumpkin, Sesame, Flax, Sunflower.

2. Hydration

  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Include coconut water, vegetable juice, electrolyte water, and herbal teas.

3. Meal Guide

  • Include vegetables (raw, cooked, or boiled).
  • Include a source of protein like curd, paneer, or tofu.
  • Replace grains with millets or seeds.
  • Avoid fried foods and beverages with meals.
  • Use Sendha Namak (rock salt) instead of white salt.

4. Diet for the Rest of the Day

  • Include seasonal fruits and nuts as snacks.
  • Drink green tea, black coffee, or red tea in moderation.
  • Include chenna or curd in your diet.
  • Avoid eating fruits after 6 PM.

5. Workout Guide

  • Avoid heavy exercises like weight lifting, long runs, trekking, and cycling.
  • Practice yoga and pranayama.
  • Stay active with household chores, walking, and light activities.

"Fasting is for giving your mind, soul, and body a rest, especially your digestive tract. Eat in moderation and keep your meals light and healthy. It's the best way to detox your body without staying hungry."
